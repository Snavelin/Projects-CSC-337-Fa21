Southwest Airlines

Southwest American

Southwest Airlines is an American domestic airline founded in 1967 by Herbert Kelleher and Rollin King and headquartered in Dallas, Texas. It is one of several major airlines in the United States and is the largest airline by domestic passengers carried. In addition to its domestic destinations, Southwest serves several Central American countries. It has a fleet size of over 700 aircraft composed entirely of Boeing 737s.
