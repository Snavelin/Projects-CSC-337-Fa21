American Airlines

Southwest American

American Airlines is an American international airline founded as a merger between Robertson Aircraft Corporation and Colonial Air Transport in 1929. It was rebranded as American Airways the following year. It currently is the largest airline in the world by fleet size at nearly 900 aircraft. Like Southwest Airlines, it is headquartered in Dallas, Texas. American serves over 50 countries worldwide.
