WONDERS OF THE WINDY CITY

This is a website about downtown Chicago and several of its famous landmark locations. This site is a work in progress, and, as such, we are constantly adding to it, so be sure to check back regularly!

Chicago, also known as the Windy City, is famous for its unique skyline, retail center, and tourist destinations. The skyline once boasted the tallest building in the world: the Sears Tower. In fact, the skyline itself is the second tallest in the United States. Since the mid 20s, the Retail District of downtown Chicago was anchored by famous department stores, such Sears, Roebuck and Co.

Famous Skyscrapers

  • Willis Tower (formerly Sears Tower)
  • John Hancock Building
  • Standard Oil Building
